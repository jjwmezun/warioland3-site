Treasure List

Throughout the game you’ll collect 100 colorful treasures. Let’s list them.

Treasures in Game Order

Treasures in Game Order
Item Location Purpose
First Music Box S1 The Grasslands Gray Opens level S2 The Big Bridge.
Second Music Box W4 A Town in Chaos Red Opens levels N4 Bank of the Wild River & N5 The Tidal Coast.
Third Music Box N6 Sea Turtle Rock Gray Opens level S5 Cave of Flames.
Fourth Music Box E1 The Stagnant Swamp Green Opens level W5 Beneath the Waves.
Fifth Music Box N1 Out of the Woods Blue Unlocks the finale @ The Temple.
Super Flippers N1 Out of the Woods Green Gives Wario the ability to swim extra fast by pressing the B button, allowing him to swim past currents. (See Controls & Abilities for mo’ info.)
Flippers S3 Tower of Revival Gray Allows Wario to swim under the surface o’ water. (See Controls & Abilities for mo’ info.)
Winged Boot E4 The Colossol Hole Green Allows Wario to jump higher by holding Up, & e’en higher by jumping off enemies. (See Controls & Abilities for mo’ info.)
Brown Glove E6 The East Crater Gray Allows Wario to pick up & throw large enemies. (See Controls & Abilities for mo’ info.)
Garlic S3 Tower of Revival Red Allows Wario to break solid blocks & kill large enemies with his charge attack. (See Controls & Abilities for mo’ info.)
Red Glove S4 The Steep Canyon Gray Allows Wario to pick up & throw small enemies. (See Controls & Abilities for mo’ info.)
Blue Overalls W2 The Volcano's Base Gray Allows Wario to ground pound. (See Controls & Abilities for mo’ info.)
Red Overalls E3 Castle of Illusions Gray Allows Wario to break solid blocks & kill large enemies with his ground pound. (See Controls & Abilities for mo’ info.)
Spike Helmet E2 The Frigid Sea Gray Allows Wario to break cracked & solid blocks by jumping under them. (See Controls & Abilities for mo’ info.)

Rain Jar N1 Out of the Woods Red Opens levels W3 The Pool of Rain & W4 A Town in Chaos by creating a cloud that rains o’er a dirt divet.
Map S3 Tower of Revival Green Opens level E6 The East Crater by showing him where it is.
Ice Spellbook N2 The Peaceful Village Red Combined with opens level E2 The Frigid Sea by freezing solid a lake.
Key S3 Tower of Revival Blue Opens level S6 Above the Clouds by unlocking a door @ the top o’ the “Tower of Revival.”
Lightning Spellbook N5 The Tidal Coast Gray Opens level S4 The Steep Canyon by creating lightning, which strikes some rocky area.
Lightning Wand N4 Bank of the Wild River Gray

Ice Wand S1 The Grasslands Green Combined with opens level E2 The Frigid Sea by freezing solid a lake.
Blue Skull W3 The Pool of Rain Red Opens level S3 Tower of Revival by making it rise from underground.

Blue Tablet Half N2 The Peaceful Village Gray Opens level W1 Desert Ruins by unlocking the door in the hill ’tween North & West.
Green Tablet Half N3 The Vast Plain Gray
Paper Fan W3 The Pool of Rain Green Opens level E1 The Stagnant Swamp by blowing ’way mist that's in the way.
Top Half o’ Scroll W1 Desert Ruins Gray Opens level W2 The Volcano's Base by, um, making a tornado disappear. Yeah, I don't get it, either.
Bottom Half o’ Scroll W2 Desert Ruins Red
Leaf W2 The Volcano's Base Blue Opens levels W6 The West Crater & E4 The Colossol Hole by causing volcano eruptions, which blasts big holes in the west side o’ the volcano & a big hole in the middle o’ the East.
Conch S4 The Steep Canyon Red
Pinecone E1 The Stagnant Swamp Red
Blue Chemicals S4 The Steep Canyon Blue Moves the 5-ton blocks in N3 The Vast Plain & W5 Beneath the Waves by spilling chemicals on the ground under them, opening access to the blue chest ’hind each o’ them.
Red Chemicals W5 Beneath the Waves Green
Pump E4 The Colossol Hole Red Makes the underwater glass cylinder in N4 Bank of the Wild River & W3 The Pool of Rain rise up to the surface, opening access to the blue chest for each o’ them.
Fruit W4 A Town in Chaos Blue Grows the octopuses in N5 The Tidal Coast & W5 Beneath the Waves, opening access to the blue chest for the former & the green chest for the latter.
Infrared Goggles E6 The East Crater Green Brightens the dark red room in N6 Sea Turtle Rock, opening access to its blue chest.
Mechanical Fan N5 The Tidal Coast Green Allows the wind to be turned on in W4 A Town in Chaos while the switch is turned on, opening access to its blue chest.
Rust Spray E3 Castle of Illusions Blue Rusts the metal walls in W6 The West Crater, S4 The Steep Canyon, & S5 Cave of Flames, opening access to the blue chest in each o’ them.
Little Knitter N4 Bank of the Wild River Green Knits the rest o’ the climbable chain walls in N5 The Tidal Coast & S3 Tower of Revival, opening access to the green chest in each o’ them.
Detonator W5 Beneath the Waves Gray Blows up the bombs attached to wooden logs in S5 Cave of Flames, E1 The Stagnant Swamp, & E4 The Colossol Hole, opening access to the green chest in S5, & the blue chest in E1 & E4.
Scissors S5 Cave of Flames Blue Cuts the string o’ the balloon @ the beginning o’ S6 Above the Clouds, opening access to its green chest.
Box W6 The West Crater Green Fills the gaps in the green & blue chest rooms o’ E3 Castle of Illusions, opening access to its blue treasure.
Remote Control E7 Forest of Fear Green Causes the yellow warp balls in E5 The Warped Void to disappear, opening access to its red & green treasures.
Red Card Key E7 Forest of Fear Blue Opens box holding Para-Goom in E5 The Warped Void, opening access to its blue treasure.
Blue Card Key E7 Forest of Fear Red
Drill S6 Above the Clouds Green Drills dirt blocking streams in E6 The East Crater, opening access to its green treasure.
Pickaxe S6 Above the Clouds Blue Picks warp hole in wall in E6 The West Crater, opening access to its blue treasure.
Toy Rocket N4 Bank of the Wild River Blue None.
Pokémon Pikachu S2 The Big Bridge Green None.
Valve E5 The Warped Void Gray Raises warp pipe in E7 Forest of Fear, opening access to its red & green treasures.
Blood E5 The Warped void Green Makes Zombies appear in graveyard area o’ E7 Forest of Fear, opening access to its blue treasure.
Sand E3 Castle of Illusions Green Blown into the big tree in N1 Out of the Woods, opening access inside o’ the tree, where the blue treasure lies.
Doll S1 The Grasslands Blue None.
Wheels S1 The Grasslands Red Gives wheels to the little cars in W2 The Volcano's Base, allowing them to move, giving Wario access to W2's red treasure.
Flute E1 The Stagnant Swamp Gray Makes the snakes appear in the pots in N2 The Peaceful Village, W2 The Volcano's Base, & S1 The Grasslands, opening access to the red & green treasures o’ N2 & the green treasures o’ W2 & S1.
Foot Weight N5 The Tidal Coast Red Shakes the earth, breaking open boulder-blocked paths in W2 The Volcano's Base & S4 The Steep Canyon & decreasing the water level o’ E1 The Stagnant Swamp. This opens access to the blue treasure o’ W2 & the red treasures o’ S4 & E1.
Left Golden Snake Eye N6 Sea Turtle Rock Red Opens the red door o’ S3 Tower of Revival, opening access to the blue treasure.
Right Golden Snake Eye W4 A Town in Chaos Green
Left Blue Snake Eye W6 The West Crater Gray Opens the blue door o’ S3 Tower of Revival, opening access to the red treasure.
Right Blue Snake Eye E4 The Colossol Hole Gray
Water Wand S5 Cave of Flames Gray Cleans up water in N6 Sea Turtle Rock, S2 The Big Bridge, & E2 The Frigid Sea, opening access to the red treasure o’ N6, the blue treasure o’ S2, & the green treasure o’ E2.
Left Half o’ Sun Stone N6 Sea Turtle Rock Green Makes the sun appear in the East, allowing it to become day there. This makes it possible to get the green treasure o’ E4 The Colossol Hole & is 1 o’ the ways to get the blue chest o’ E2 The Frigid Sea.
Right Half o’ Sun Stone W1 Desert Ruins Blue
Whirlwind E1 The Stagnant Swamp Blue Combined with , makes leaves fall in the lake area o’ N1 Out of the Woods, opening access to its green treasure.
Seed S2 The Big Bridge Gray Drops seeds in N3 The Vast Plain, W3 The Pool of Rain, & S1 The Grasslands, granting access to the red treasures o’ W3 & S1 & the green treasure o’ N3.
Sack S5 Cave of Flames Green Combined with , makes leaves fall in the lake area o’ N1 Out of the Woods, opening access to its green treasure.
Gong N6 Sea Turtle Rock Blue Makes the moon come out during night in S6 Above the Clouds, opening access to its blue treasure.
Telephone S4 The Steep Canyon Green None.
